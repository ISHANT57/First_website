*Communication and Ethics*

Course Overview (Ist Semester)

This course covers the principles of effective communication and ethical practices in personal and professional settings.

Topics Covered

  • Effective Communication
    1. Verbal and Non-Verbal Communication
    2. Active Listening
    3. Public Speaking Skills
  • Ethics in Communication
    1. Oral Presentations
    2. Ethical Decision Making
